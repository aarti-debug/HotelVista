Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation through RoyalNest?

You can book directly through our Booking page, call our concierge line at +91 98765 43210, or send us an email at reservations@royalnest.in. Our team will confirm your booking within 2 hours.

Do you offer special packages for honeymoons or anniversaries?

Yes! We have exclusive romance packages including rose petal turndown, candlelit dinners, couple spa sessions, and complimentary champagne. Contact us to design your perfect celebration.

Is airport transfer included with all bookings?

Complimentary luxury car airport transfers are included with all bookings of 2 nights or more. For single-night stays, transfers can be arranged at a nominal charge.

What is the cancellation and refund policy?

Cancellations made 7+ days before arrival receive a full refund. Cancellations within 3–6 days incur a 25% fee. Within 48 hours of arrival, the first night is non-refundable. Please refer to our full policy page for details.

Can I arrange corporate or group bookings?

Absolutely. We offer tailored corporate rates, event packages, and group booking discounts for 10 or more rooms. Email our corporate desk at corporate@royalnest.in for a custom proposal.

Are the hotels pet-friendly?

Pet policies vary by property. Kumarakom Lake Resort and The Leela Goa welcome small pets with prior notice. Please contact us before booking to confirm arrangements for your furry companion.
