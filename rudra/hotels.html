Udaipur, Rajasthan

Taj Lake Palace

Lake Pichola, Udaipur 313001

Built in 1746 by Maharana Jagat Singh II, the Taj Lake Palace is an ethereal vision of white marble rising from the shimmering waters of Lake Pichola. This floating palace hotel offers 83 exquisitely decorated rooms and suites, each reflecting the opulence of the Mewar dynasty. Dining here means feasting on traditional Rajasthani cuisine under starlit skies with the lake as your backdrop.

Mumbai, Maharashtra

Taj Mahal Palace

Apollo Bunder, Mumbai 400001

Since 1903, the Taj Mahal Palace has been the crown jewel of Mumbai's skyline. Standing majestically opposite the Gateway of India, this iconic Victorian Gothic and Moorish Revival landmark has hosted royalty, heads of state and legends. With 285 luxuriously appointed rooms, acclaimed restaurants and a legendary heritage, it remains the ultimate address in Mumbai.

#4 Best in India🏯
Jaipur, Rajasthan

Rambagh Palace

Bhawani Singh Rd, Jaipur 302005

Once the hunting lodge of the Maharaja of Jaipur, the magnificent Rambagh Palace was transformed into the most celebrated royal residence in Rajasthan. Today it stands as a Taj hotel and is often called the "Jewel of Jaipur." The palace is surrounded by 47 acres of immaculate Mughal gardens, polo grounds and opulent royal suites that transport guests to a bygone era of Indian royalty.

Goa

The Leela Goa

Mobor, Cavelossim, Goa 403731
